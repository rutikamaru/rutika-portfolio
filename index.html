about me

I’m Rutika, a designer who believes every website should feel like an experience, not just a page on the internet. With a blend of creativity and technical skill, I design clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces that make brands stand out while staying easy to use.

My focus areas include:

  • UI/UX Design – creating designs that are intuitive and visually engaging
  • Responsive Layouts – making sure websites look perfect on every screen
  • Brand-Driven Design – reflecting each brand’s unique identity in the digital space
  • Interactive Details – adding subtle animations and elements that keep users engaged

When I’m not designing, I turn to art and travel for fresh ideas and inspiration. My goal is simple: to craft designs that not only look beautiful but also connect with people.
